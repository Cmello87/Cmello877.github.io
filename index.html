Tilapia Fish

  • Time:30 Minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Tilapia Fish 4-6
Lemons 3
Black Pepper 2 teaspoons
Butter 2 tablespoons

Step 1: Mix your black pepper with the 2 lemons, place the fish fillets in the prepped sauce and leave in there for 15 minutes to soak up the juice and flavors.leave 1 Lemon for baking later.

Step 2: Take the Butter place in microwave for 30 seconds , once melted transfer to a oven safe dish

Step 3: Now slice the remaining lemon into circled thick slices

Step4: Place fish fillets in the oven safe dish and place sliced lemons on top of the fish, place in oven at 350 degrees for 15 minutes and enjoy!

Spaghetti

  • Time: 25 Minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti Noodles 1 Barrilla Package
Ground Beef 1 lb
Black Pepper 2 teaspoons
Chili flakes 1 tablespoons
Prego Sauce 1 whole Jar
Parmesan Cheese 2 cups

Step 1: Boil the Barilla Pasta for 12 minutes in a deep pan until "al dente"

Step 2: Brown the ground beef in a pan over medium heat for about 8 minutes throw in black pepper and Chili flakes towards end of cooking, set aside.

Step 3:Drain the Noodles in a colander, then throw the meat and pasta into a pan and mix at medium temperature

Step4: Add Prego sauce mix well and finish off with the parmesan cheese turn heat off and enjoy !
